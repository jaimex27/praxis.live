video:time-fx:difference

This component calculates the difference between the current frame and the previous one. It predates video:analysis:difference, and is equivalent to feeding the same source into that component with a video:analysis:frame-delay on one channel.

Supports three modes - Color, Mono and Threshold (black or white). Differences less than the threshold value are ignored in all modes and come out black.
