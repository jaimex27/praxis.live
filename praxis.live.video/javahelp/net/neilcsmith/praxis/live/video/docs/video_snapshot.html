video:snapshot

This component provides the ability to capture and display a still frame from its video input. It extends this ability with the option to fade from the previous captured image to the new one over a period of time, and to mix the new image with the previous one.

Ports

in
video in | Video signal to capture from.
out
video out | Video output signal.
in
video in | Video signal to capture from.
fade-time
control in | number(0..3600) | Period in seconds to fade from previous image to current image.
mix
control in | number(0..1) | Level to mix current image with previous image.
trigger
control in | any | Trigger new snapshot.
reset
control in | any | Clear snapshot.

Controls

fade-time
property | number(0..3600) | 0 | Period in seconds to fade from previous image to current image.
mix
property | number(0..1) | 1 | Level to mix current image with previous image.
trigger
function | - | - | Trigger new snapshot.
reset
function | - | - | Clear snapshot.
