video:mix:xfader

Cross fade between two video signals, using the blend mode set.

Available blend modes are Normal, Add, Difference, BitXor

Ports

in-1
video in | First video input signal.
in-2
video in | Second video input signal.
out
video out | Video output signal.
mix
control in | number(0..1) | Mix amount (0 all in-1, 1 all in-2
mode
control in | string(in modes) | Blend mode to use during cross fade.

Controls

mix
property | number(0..1) | 0 | Mix amount (0 all in-1, 1 all in-2
mode
property | string(in modes) | Normal | Blend mode to use during cross fade.
