video:mix:composite

Compose the video input source onto the video input destination using the blend mode and additional opacity as set.

The blend modes available are Normal, Add, Sub, Difference, Multiply, Screen, BitXor

Ports

in
video in | Video input signal.
src
video in | Video input to be composed on top of in
out
video out | Video output signal.
mode
control in | string(from blend modes) | Blend mode to use.
mix
control in | number(0..1) | Opacity of src

Controls

force-alpha
property | boolean | false | Force src to have an alpha channel, even if output doesn't (defaults to same as output).
mode
property | string(from blend modes) | Normal | Blend mode to use.
mix
property | number(0..1) | 1 | Opacity of src
