audio:modulation:lfo-delay

A mono delay with delay time controlled by a low frequency oscillator.

This is similar to audio:modulation:chorus but with a wider range of parameters.

Ports

ID Type Direction Values Description
in audio in Audio input.
out audio out Audio output.
time control in number(0..1) Delay time (average) in seconds.
range control in number(0..1) Depth of LFO (in proportion of delay time).
rate control in number(0..40) Rate of LFO (in Hertz).
feedback control in number(0..1) Amount of signal fed back into input.
mix control in number(0..1) Mix.
