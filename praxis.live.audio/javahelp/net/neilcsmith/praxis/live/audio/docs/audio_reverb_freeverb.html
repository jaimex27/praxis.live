audio:reverb:freeverb

A stereo reverb, port of the popular Freeverb.

Ports

ID Type Direction Values Description
in-1 audio in Audio input.
in-2 audio in Audio input.
out-1 audio out Audio output.
out-2 audio out Audio output.
room-size control in number(0..1) Room size.
damp control in number(0..1) Damping.
width control in number(0..1) Stereo width.
wet control in number(0..1) Level of reverberation signal.
dry control in number(0..1) Level of original signal (0.5 is original gain).
